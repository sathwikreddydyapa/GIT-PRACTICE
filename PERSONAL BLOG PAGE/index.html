LORD VINAYAKA

Lord Vinayaka, the elephant-headed deity, is revered as the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom and intellect. He is worshipped at the start of all new ventures to ensure success and is the beloved son of Shiva and Parvati. His large ears symbolize his ability to listen to all prayers and his wisdom.

HAPPY GANESH CHATURTHI

On Ganesh Chaturthi, devotees install a beautifully decorated idol of Lord Ganesha in their homes or a public pandal. Throughout the festival, they perform daily prayers and aarti (a ritual of worship with light), offering his favorite sweets like modak. The festival concludes with the Visarjan, where the idol is immersed in a body of water, symbolizing his return to Mount Kailash.
